# Hekhalot, Kundalini & vision traditions — comparative timeline

This dataset maps the **long parallel history** of two mystical systems — Jewish **Hekhalot/Merkabah** ascent mysticism and Hindu/Tantric **Kundalini yoga** — against the Hebrew Bible and New Testament theophany/vision episodes that bracket and inform the Hekhalot tradition, and against Islamic, Kabbalistic, and Buddhist structural analogues.

**How to read this table.** Each row belongs to one of five broad `Tradition_System` streams:

| Stream | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **OT Vision** | Hebrew Bible theophanic and prophetic vision episodes (Genesis → Daniel) |
| **Hekhalot/Merkabah** | Second Temple apocalyptic literature, Hekhalot texts, and their medieval descendants |
| **NT Vision** | New Testament visionary episodes (Baptism, Transfiguration, Paul, Revelation) |
| **Kundalini/Subtle Body** | South Asian subtle-body traditions from Vedic prana concepts to classical Kundalini yoga |
| **Cross-Traditional** | Structural comparison rows and explicit scholarly parallels across streams |

The `Evidence_Tier` column follows the site's [standard tier scheme](method-evidence-and-influence.md):

- **Tier A** — well-attested; mainstream scholarly consensus
- **Tier B** — plausible; debated details or reconstructed dates
- **Tier C** — structural or speculative parallel; no established genealogy

The final row ("Seven Stages: Hekhalot Halls vs. Seven Chakras vs. Seven Sefirot") is explicitly **Tier C** — a structural observation, not a claim of historical influence.

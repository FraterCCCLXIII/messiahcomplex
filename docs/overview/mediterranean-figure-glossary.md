# Glossary: Adonis, Sabazius, Melqart, Verethraghna

Short **orientation** entries for figures marked **Tier C** on [Eurasian mediator figures assessment](eurasian-mediator-figures-assessment.md). Not full articles; use **specialist** **literature** before citing in scholarship.

## Adonis

**Syrian–Levantine** **vegetation** **beautiful** **youth** (**Byblos**–**Aphek** myths; **Greek** **spread** via **Cyprus** and **Athens**). **Women’s** **ritual** (**Adōnia**); **death** in **boar** hunt and **partial** **return** from **underworld**. **Typological** neighbor to **Osiris** / **Attis**; **less** **cosmic** **institutional** **cult** than **Isis** or **Cybele** in **most** **imperial** **cities**.

## Sabazius

**Thraco**-**Phrygian**-associated **deity** often **linked** in **ancient** **polemic** to **Dionysus**; **iconography** includes **rider** **hand** with **serpent**. **Initiatory** **formula** *euai sabai* in **literary** **sources**. Overlap with **Dionysiac** **milieu** and **regional** **meter** cults — see [Greek wandering gods](greek-wandering-gods-initiation.md), [Thracian horseman](thracian-horseman-paleo-balkan.md). Chronological situating of hero vs mystery institutions: [Hero cults and mystery cults — Mediterranean atlas](hero-cult-and-mystery-cult-atlas.md).

## Melqart

**Tyrian** **dynastic** / **city** god; **patron** of **colonies** (**Carthage** **strand**). **Euhemerized** connections to **Heracles** in **Greek** **interpretatio**. **Kingship** and **dynastic** **mediation** more **salient** than **individual** mystery initiation for **thesis** purposes.

## Vərəθraγna / Verethraghna

**Avestan** **yazata** of **victory**, **epithet** cluster and **animal** **manifestations** (**bird**, **boar**, etc.). **Warrior**-**mediation** and **royal** **ideology** in **Iranian** **religion**; **not** a **Mediterranean** mystery **initiator**, but useful for **Indo-Iranian** **side** of **comparative** **savior** **imagery** — [Indo-Iranian Mitra and the contract complex](indo-iranian-mitra-contract.md), [Mithra cluster](/docs/mithras).

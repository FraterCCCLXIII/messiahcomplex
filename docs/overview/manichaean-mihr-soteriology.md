# Manichaean Mihr and soteriology

**Mani** (3rd cent. CE) organized **salvation** within a **dualistic** **myth** of **light** **particles** **trapped** in **matter** and **released** through **knowledge**, **ascetic** **discipline**, and **liturgy**. **Mihr** (**Mithra**) appears among **light** beings in **Manichaean** **Middle** **Persian**, **Parthian**, **Sogdian**, **Uighur**, and related **texts**—a **distinct** **synthesis** from **Roman** **tauroctony** **Mithras** or **Avestan** **liturgy** alone.

## What is relatively secure

- **Lieu**, *Manichaeism in Central Asia and China* (Brill) and **corpus** **editions** document **Mihr** in **salvific** **frameworks** across **Iranian** and **Turkic** **Manichaean** **communities**.
- **Manichaeism** is **trans-regional**: **Mesopotamia**, **Iran**, **Central** **Asia**, **China**; **text** **survival** is **uneven** (often **anti**-**heretical** **filters** or **dry** **climates**).
- **Azimia (2023)** — "Mithra in Light of Buddhism and Manichaean: Savior Figure" (*Royal College of Surgeons in Ireland*) — draws on the Manichaean Mihr figure as a light-bearer, judge, and guide to argue that **Maitreya and Mithra are the same figure**. This is a **minority / Tier C** position; it usefully maps the Manichaean Mihr's soteriological role but the identity claim is not accepted in mainstream scholarship. Use Lieu and primary Manichaean corpus editions as the reliable anchor; Azimia (2023) as orientation only.

## What to avoid

- **Collapsing** **Manichaean** **Mihr** into **Roman** **Mithras** **initiation** grades or **Avestan** **Yašt** 10 **without** **text**-specific argument.
- **Equating** **Mihr** with **Maitreya** on **phonetic** or **typological** **hunches** alone—see skepticism flagged on [Amitābha, Maitreya, and Gandhāra transmission](amitabha-maitreya-gandhara-transmission.md) and [Measuring influence: Mitra, Mithra, Maitreya](measuring-influence-mitra-maitreya.md).

## Relation to this site

**Manichaean** **Mihr** belongs on the **map** as an **Iranian**-**tinged** **light** **mediator** inside a **revealed** **cosmology**—**family** **resemblance** to other **savior** **discourses**, **separate** **historical** **bundle** from **Jewish** **messiah** or **Buddhist** **bodhisattva** ** vows**.

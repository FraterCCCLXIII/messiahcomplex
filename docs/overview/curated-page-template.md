# Curated page template

Use this **pattern** for **overview** and **figure** pages so each doc answers *why it exists*, *what is stable*, *what is debated*, and *where to read next*. Copy the block below into new pages; remove sections that do not apply.

**Worked example:** [Method: evidence, influence, and milieu](method-evidence-and-influence.md) models tiered claims and explicit limits.

---

## Template (copy from here)

```markdown
---
title: …
description: …
sidebar_position: …
---

# Page title

## Context

- **Audience:** Who should read this (e.g. comparativists, students of tradition X, readers tracking influence claims).
- **Question:** What decision or confusion this page helps with.
- **Scope:** What the page deliberately does *not* do (non-goals).

## Consensus snapshot (or “Relatively stable description”)

Short paragraph(s) on what specialists broadly accept—dates, languages, main textual witnesses—before debate.

## Debates and tiers

Bullets or short paragraphs labeled with evidence **tier** (see [Method](method-evidence-and-influence.md)): secure description, specialist debate, hypothesis, analogy only.

## See also

3–8 internal links to hubs and siblings.

## Sources & dating

| Witness / work | Language | Approx. date | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| … | … | … | Editions, standard translations, caveats |

### Secondary entrances (optional)

2–4 monographs, handbook chapters, or encyclopedia articles (full bibliographic lines).

---

Raw research dumps belong in `docs/research/` with a link from the curated page; see [About research notes](../research/about-research-notes.md).
```

## Shared context (avoid repeating on every leaf)

Long **milieu** blocks (e.g. civic religion in Roman Philippi) should live on **one** overview page; leaf pages **link** there instead of duplicating. Hubs: [Messianic archetypes](messianic-archetypes.md), [Mithra cluster hub](/docs/mithras), [Gandhara and Central Asian transmission](gandhara-central-asian-transmission.md), [Philippi / Via Egnatia milieu](philippi-via-egnatia-religious-milieu.md).
